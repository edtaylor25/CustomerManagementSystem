Customer Manager

Hello and welcome to our Custmer Management System! A quick, easy application to keep track of all customers in your buisness to help keep buisness organized and flowing. Click "Register" in the upper right tab to add a new Customer! Search any customer card by name or state to filter details. Click "update" on customer cards to change any of their information. Simply click "unregister" to delete a customer from the system.
